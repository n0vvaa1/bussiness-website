About Us

Vertex Exterior
Cleaning

We're a Croydon North-based exterior cleaning business built on one principle: leave every surface better than we found it.

What sets us apart is our commitment to using the right tools for the job. We operate professional-grade pressure washing equipment that delivers deep, effective cleaning on driveways and hard surfaces — achieving results that simply aren't possible with standard equipment.

Alongside our pressure washing capabilities, we use high-quality cleaning tools and surface-appropriate products on every job — ensuring a safe, thorough clean that won't damage your paintwork, glass, pavers or surfaces.

We're a friendly, professional team that takes pride in every detail. Whether it's your daily driver, your front driveway, or your home windows — we care about the result as much as you do.
